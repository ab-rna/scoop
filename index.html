About Me

I am a thirteen year old Scoop McLaren, detective and reporter, on a mission to solve the mystery of Sonny Fink with my best friend Evie. I live in Higgity Harbour, and I am determined to find out more about Sonny Fink to save our city, and to show my father that I was born to be a journalist. I run my own online news service for my town, trying to be resourceful of what i have to use. I always have my pen and paper pad on me at all times!

Sonny Fink

Sonny Fink is an evil woman who writes a newspaper every day at midnight. But theres one twist, since whatever she writes in it comes true. Shes made the city rain frogs, and even turned people into stone. Me and Evie are on a mission to find her, and I will not back down.
